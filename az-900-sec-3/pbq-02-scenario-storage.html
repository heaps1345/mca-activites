Scenario: Which Storage Tier?

AZ-900 Section 3 · Scenario

A hospital needs to store seven years of patient scan images to meet regulatory requirements. The images are accessed during the first 30 days after creation but are almost never viewed again after that. Storage costs must be minimised, and the organisation accepts that retrieving old images may take several hours.

Which Blob Storage access tier should be used for images older than 30 days?

A. Hot — for frequent access and fastest retrieval
B. Cool — for infrequent access with lower storage costs
C. Archive — for rarely accessed data with lowest storage cost
D. Premium — for highest performance storage
