Scenario: Enforcing Governance

AZ-900 Section 4 · Scenario

A company wants to ensure that no Azure resources can be deployed outside of UK South and UK West regions. This rule must apply automatically to all subscriptions under their management group, and any deployment to a non-compliant region should be blocked — not just flagged.

Which Azure governance feature should they use?

A. Azure Blueprints — to template the compliant configuration
B. Azure Policy with a Deny effect — to block non-compliant deployments
C. RBAC — to restrict who can deploy resources
D. Resource Locks — to prevent modification of existing resources
